ABOUT US

Established in 1992 as a software house; SOFCOM is engaged in the field of business applications, software development and support.

For over 20 years of flawless dedication and experience, our company has excelled in providing innovative and intelligent solutions in the focused business domains of Human Capital Management (HARMONY) and Laboratory Information Management Solution (SPECTRUM). These applications have been designed to cater the need of customers spread over diversified industrial segments and businesses, helping us and our clientage sync and achieve impossible tasks.

With our solutions, you can depict your business processes and policies in detail to get intelligent controls and in-depth information on performance. We provide you with unmatched speed and flexibility in our solutions.

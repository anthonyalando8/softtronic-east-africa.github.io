ABOUT US

Established in 1992 as a software house; SOFTTRONIC is engaged in the field of business applications, software development and support.

For over 20 years of flawless dedication and experience, our company has excelled in providing innovative and intelligent solutions in the focused business domains of Human Capital Management (HARMONY) and Laboratory Information Management Solution (SPECTRUM). These applications have been designed to cater the need of customers spread over diversified industrial segments and businesses, helping us and our clientage sync and achieve impossible tasks.

With our solutions, you can depict your business processes and policies in detail to get intelligent controls and in-depth information on performance. We provide you with unmatched speed and flexibility in our solutions.

OUR APPROACH

Technology has made it possible to do more with less; we cannot predict where it will take us in the future. But we do believe that by handling specific details of business processes we can best shape the future.

SOFTTRONIC’s team has always been instrumental to its customer’s growth. This emphasizes our approach towards customer solution efficacy.

Since the inception of SOFTTRONIC, we have embarked ourselves on a journey where we have not lost a single customer for lack of performance or application efficiency. SOFTTRONIC has a dedicated team of trained and experienced professionals, focused entirely on minute details of your business processes, and passionate about understanding customer needs.
